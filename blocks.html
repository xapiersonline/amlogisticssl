High-Quality Blocks from AM Logistics

At AM Logistics, we take pride in manufacturing a variety of high-quality concrete blocks designed to meet the needs of diverse construction projects. Whether you’re building residential homes, commercial structures, or large-scale infrastructure, our blocks are engineered for strength, durability, and ease of installation.

  • Hollow concrete blocks are known for their lightweight structure, making them easy to handle and ideal for walls.
  • Solid concrete blocks are robust and highly durable, providing excellent stability for large construction projects.
  • Aerated concrete blocks are made with air bubbles, giving them a lightweight nature and improved insulation properties.
  • Our paving blocks are designed specifically for outdoor use in creating durable pavements, driveways, and other landscaping applications.
  • For projects requiring unique designs, we also provide custom-shaped blocks, including curved blocks, which are used for arches, landscaping borders, and decorative walls.

Hollow Concrete Blocks

Specifications:

  • Size: Available in 4”, 6”, and 8” thickness options
  • Density: High density for durability
  • Compressive Strength: Designed to withstand heavy loads

Uses:

  • Load-Bearing Walls: Suitable for internal and external load-bearing walls in both residential and commercial buildings
  • Thermal Insulation: Provides additional insulation in climates with varying temperatures
  • Sound Barrier Walls: Often used in buildings requiring soundproofing, such as schools or offices

Solid Concrete Blocks:

Specifications:

  • Size: Available in multiple sizes, commonly 4” and 8” thicknesses
  • Density: Extremely high density for added durability
  • Compressive Strength: Built to withstand high compressive loads

Uses:

  • Foundations: Ideal for constructing solid foundations in residential, commercial, and industrial projects
  • Heavy Load-Bearing Structures: Suitable for load-bearing walls and columns in multi-story buildings
  • Pavements and Landscaping: Often used in pathways, pavements, and landscaping due to their sturdiness

Aerated (Lightweight) Concrete Blocks

Specifications:

  • Size: Available in 4”, 6”, and 8” thicknesses
  • Weight: Lightweight, reducing the overall load on structures
  • Thermal Insulation: High insulation value due to air bubbles

Uses:

  • Partitions and Non-Load-Bearing Walls: Commonly used in interior walls and partition walls where thermal and sound insulation is required
  • Multi-Story Constructions: Ideal for non-load-bearing walls in high-rise buildings to reduce structural load
  • Energy-Efficient Buildings: Provides effective insulation for buildings aiming for energy efficiency

Paving Blocks

Specifications:

  • Shapes: Rectangular, interlocking, and custom designs
  • Thickness: Typically 60mm to 80mm, depending on usage requirements
  • Load Capacity: High load-bearing capacity for vehicular traffic

Uses:

  • Driveways and Parking Lots: Ideal for residential and commercial driveways, offering a long-lasting and visually appealing solution
  • Walkways and Gardens: Suitable for landscaping projects, pathways, and public gardens
